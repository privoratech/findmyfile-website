FindMyFile Account Deletion Policy

Last updated: July 19, 2025

This page explains how you can request the deletion of your FindMyFile account and all associated data.

How to Request Account Deletion

You can request the deletion of your account directly from within the FindMyFile app:

  1. Open the FindMyFile app and navigate to the settings screen (by clicking "Select Folders to Search").
  2. Tap the "Delete My Account" button.
  3. A confirmation dialog will appear, explaining that this action is permanent and will cancel any active subscription.
  4. Confirm your choice to submit the deletion request.

What Happens Next?

Once your request is submitted, your account will be added to a deletion queue. The final deletion will be processed in a batch within 24-48 hours. The following data will be permanently removed:

  • Your account from Firebase Authentication (including email and login credentials).
  • Your user document from our database, which includes your subscription status and expiry date.

This action cannot be undone.
